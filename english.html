Bio

I am a professional Web Designer and Web Developer since 2005.

I work at IFRN, where I'm responsible for some web projects. And I am a partner at Soluti company, where I'm technical responsible for web designer projects.

I have experience in more than 60 projects, most of them institutional sites and systems to MEC and IFRN, working with both programming and design, and as a consultant.

My development style is to keep a logical and efficient code structure, always in balance with the web standards, and searching the best screens results for the user.
